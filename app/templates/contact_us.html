Q: Do I need to sign up to buy tickets? A: No. While signing up is quick and free, TheaterHub wants to create an easy experience for all users. You will still need to provide an e-mail for a receipt, however, we do not save that information unless you are logged in. Q: How do I buy tickets? A: Click on the 'Buy Tickets' tab, and then type in a movie's name or genre into the search bar. Click on the movie you'd like to see. Once you've confirmed the information is correct, type in any asked information and click "Checkout", and it should be ready to buy. Q: How do I create an account? A: Click on 'log in' and then click 'Register'. Fill out the form there, and hit 'submit' to register. If you are successful, you should be able to log in.
