Q: Do I need to sign up to buy tickets? A: No. While signing up is quick and free, TheaterHub wants to create an easy experience for all users. You will still need to provide an e-mail for a receipt, however, we do not save that information unless you are logged in. Q: How do I buy tickets? A: Click on the 'Buy Tickets' tab, and then type in a movie's name or genre into the search bar. Click on the movie you'd like to see, then click on the movie theater you'd like to see it at. You should then see the time slots the movies are playing at. Click on the one you want, and type in the quantity of tickets you'd like to buy, and hit confirm. Once that is done, you should see a summary of the tickets you're buying. Once you've confirmed the information is correct, type in any asked information and click "Checkout". {% endblock %}
